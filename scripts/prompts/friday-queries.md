# Friday Queries - Week in Review & Weekend Ahead

SECTION: Key Headlines
List the 3-5 most important global news stories from the PAST 48 HOURS ONLY. Prioritize stories from AP, Reuters, NPR, BBC, or major newspapers. CRITICAL: For any casualty/death toll figures, cite the specific source and note if figures are preliminary. Do NOT include stories older than 2 days. Format MUST be bullet points starting with • and bold headline:
• **Headline**: Brief summary with source attribution.

SECTION: Week in Review
List the top 5-7 most significant stories from the past week (Monday-Friday). Format MUST be bullet points starting with • and bold headline:
• **Headline**: Brief summary of how the story developed.

SECTION: Weekend Culture & Entertainment
List upcoming weekend events, entertainment options, cultural activities in Connecticut and the region. Include concerts, theater, festivals, exhibitions, notable film/streaming releases. Format MUST be bullet points starting with • and bold headline:
• **Event/Activity Name**: Brief description with location/platform and dates.

SECTION: Hacker News Weekly Top Stories

• **Story Title**: Brief description of the discussion.
