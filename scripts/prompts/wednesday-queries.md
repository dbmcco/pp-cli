# Wednesday Queries - Culture & Retrospective

SECTION: Key Headlines
List the 3-5 most important global news stories from the PAST 48 HOURS ONLY. Prioritize stories from AP, Reuters, NPR, BBC, or major newspapers. CRITICAL: For any casualty/death toll figures, cite the specific source and note if figures are preliminary. Do NOT include stories older than 2 days. Format MUST be bullet points starting with • and bold headline:
• **Headline**: Brief summary with source attribution.

SECTION: This Week in History
List 3-4 significant historical events that happened the past week (November 4-10) in different years. Include year and brief context. Format MUST be bullet points starting with • and bold headline:
• **Year - Event Name**: Brief description and historical significance.

SECTION: Arts & Culture
List 3-5 recent developments in arts, music, film, theater, or cultural events. Include new releases, exhibitions, performances. Format MUST be bullet points starting with • and bold headline:
• **Event/Release Name**: Brief description.

SECTION: Connecticut Culture & Events
List 2-3 cultural events, festivals, or entertainment happenings in Connecticut the past week or UPCOMING. Format MUST be bullet points starting with • and bold headline:
• **Event Name**: Brief description with location and dates.
